Autobiography


My name is Tsangyu Lin, I was born in Taichung City, Taiwan in 2000

Currently I am major in software engineering in Tamkang University, planning in 2021 Winter Semester to be an exchange student in LMU Munich with same faculty. I can speak 4 languages, which are English, German, and Mandarin Chinese.

I was born in a average-wealth family, with my sister and my father in Taipei. During the workday, I am in the student dormitory in Tamkang university Lanyang campus, which is located in Yilan. I am enthusiastic with programming since I was 9 year old, it all started when my mother's collegue gave me a book called "Fundamentals of Visual Basic 6.0". Since that day I had been trying every examples in the book, and after few days, instead of just copying from the book directly, I can gradually modify the code to the way I wanted to. Then the very first program I had ever been created, it's called "An easy game of basic math."

After several years, I started to experience different fields in programming language, including C++, C, Java (which is my best programming language so far), Python, and so forth.

And I love to play it so much, even abandoned my whole school work. Everytime I come back home, the first thing I want to do is just play Minecraft. And after I got bored of the single player, I opened a server to invite more friends to play with me. And it is the start of a catastrophe. Because the day I opened the server, I noticed that even if I created the server, most people wouldn’t know it very much.
