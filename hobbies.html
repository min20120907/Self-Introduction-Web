My Hobbies

Most of my hobbies are related to music listening, bike riding, jogging, hiking, and of course the programming.

At the year 2016, I am diagnosed as an high-blood pressure patient, which is because not only I was born in VSD (aka Ventricular Septal Defect), but also the severe obese problem on my overweight body.

So the doctor demanded me to get myself fit. Then I decided to do sport, to make my self healthier. After months logging my calories

The greatest achievement in my whole life is that I have lost over 30 kilograms. Why am I losing weight? Because I said last year that if I don't lose it, my heart problem will worsen since I was born. And after losing weight, not only have I used to be healthier now, but I've also had a sports habit. Of course I do a lot of sports and I use an application called MyFitnessPal to log my meals. Though I become 5 more kilograms than the day I stopped logging, I still go cycling and logging them on Strava. Now I start logging them again. Hope it will have some great results.

Also, my favorite sport is cycling and running on a treadmill. Because both sports the distance can be logged. This way I can get a lot of GIS activities, and get achievements from them. I have visited many places with my giant bike, for example, from Songshan Railway Station to Taoyuan Airport, or from Chung Yuan University to Taipei Railway Station. And the longest journey is from my home in Taipei to my university, Tamkang University Lanyang Campus. During the excursion you can see many colorful views near the Pacific Ocean.
